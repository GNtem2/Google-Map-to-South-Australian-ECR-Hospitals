Welcome to the simulated maps for emergency clot retrieval stroke services provision in metropolitan Adelaide. These interactive maps display the region serviced by a hospital under various service models - i.e. number of hospitals - discussed in supplementary material of the paper (see citation link above). Links at the top of this page allow access to maps displaying different service models. Please note that these maps were based on estimates of travel time from random addresses to the major hospitals using Google Maps and were not based on real ambulance transits. Also note that the study was performed before relocation of the Royal Adelaide Hospital to new premises.
